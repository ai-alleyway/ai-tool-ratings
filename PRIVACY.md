# Privacy Policy — AI Tool Ratings

_Last updated: 2026-07-01_

**AI Tool Ratings** ("the extension"), published by AI Alleyway, is built to collect nothing. This
policy explains exactly what it does and does not do.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** The extension does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information or
usage data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking pixels, and no third-party scripts.

## How it works

- The tool ratings are bundled inside the extension as a static data file, so searching works entirely
  on your device — even offline.
- The extension requests **no browser permissions**: no access to your open tabs, your browsing
  history, your data on websites, or your identity.
- When you click a review (or use the `aa` address-bar search), the extension opens the corresponding
  page on `aialleyway.com` in a new tab. That is a normal page visit to our website; it is not tracking
  performed by the extension. The link carries a UTM tag so we can count, in aggregate, how many visits
  came from the extension — it contains no information about you.

## Data sharing

We share no data because we collect no data.

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "last updated" date.
