# Privacy Policy — AI Tool Ratings

_Last updated: 2026-07-01_

**AI Tool Ratings** ("the extension"), published by AI Alleyway, is built to collect nothing. This
policy explains exactly what it does and does not do.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** The extension does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information or
usage data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking pixels, and no third-party scripts.

## How it works

- The catalog (reviews, picks, and guides) ships bundled inside the extension, so searching works on
  your device — even offline.
- To stay current, the extension fetches an updated **public catalog file** from our GitHub Pages host
  (`ai-alleyway.github.io`). This is an ordinary request for a public static file — the same kind your
  browser makes when loading any web page, and it necessarily includes your IP address as every web
  request does. We do **not** use it to identify, profile, or track you, and it carries no information
  about you. If the request fails (e.g. offline), the bundled copy is used.
- The extension requests **no browser permissions**: no access to your open tabs, your browsing
  history, your data on websites, or your identity.
- When you click a result (or use the `aa` address-bar search), the extension opens the corresponding
  page on `aialleyway.com` in a new tab. That is a normal page visit to our website, not tracking
  performed by the extension. The link carries a UTM tag so we can count, in aggregate, how many visits
  came from the extension — it contains no information about you.

## Data sharing

We share no data because we collect no data.
